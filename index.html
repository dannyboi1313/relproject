Jesus Christ

Savior, Redeemer, Prince of Peace

Who I am

Before the world was, I AM. I am Jesus Christ, the Son of the living God. I am the resurrection and the life. I created the heavens and the earth, and all things that in them are. In Me Hath the Father Glorified His Name. I Am the Light and the Life of the World, and whosever believeth in me, shall have eternal life.

My Mission

I doeth not anything save it be for the benefit of the world; for I loveth the world, even that I layeth down my own life that I might draw all men unto me. I suffered so that I might know how to succor my people. It is my work and my glory to bring to pass the immortality of man.

Why me?

Because there is no other name under heaven given among men by which we must be saved. Your testimony of me will influence which kingdom of glory you recieve.
